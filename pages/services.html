Our Institutes

MCAST provides top-tier vocational education through its specialized Institutes, offering a wide range of courses from Applied Sciences to Creative Arts:

  • Institute of Applied Sciences
  • Institute for the Creative Arts
  • Institute of Engineering and Transport
  • Institute of Business Management and Commerce
  • Institute of Community Services
  • Institute of Information and Communication Technology
  • Gozo Campus
  • The New Institute for the Trades

These institutes provide all the technical and professional expertise towards the delivery of the programmes at MCAST, maintaining a healthy dialogue with all interested stakeholders to provide the best programmes for the needs of the local economy and society.
