The Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology

Established in 2001, the Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology is the country's leading vocational education and training institution. MCAST offers over 190 full-time courses and over 300 part-time vocational courses ranging from certificates to Doctoral level (MQF Level 1 to Level 8).

Our mission is to provide universally accessible vocational and professional education and training with an international dimension, responsive to the needs of the individual and the economy.
